Architecture Principles & Prime Day Scaling

During events like Prime Day, the platform leverages Auto Scaling, CloudFront caching, and SQS decoupling to absorb massive traffic spikes smoothly. Traffic flows from Route 53 -> CloudFront/WAF -> ALB -> Compute (EC2/EKS/Lambda) -> Data (Aurora/DynamoDB).

Note: This is a learning architecture illustrating common AWS patterns for a large Amazon-like commerce platform, not an official Amazon internal diagram.
